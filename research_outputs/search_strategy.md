# Search Strategy: Atrial Fibrillation in Cardiology

## Research Topic Analysis
**Primary Topic**: Atrial fibrillation (AF)
**Domain**: Cardiology
**Search Date**: 2025-11-09

## Database-Specific Search Strategies

### PubMed/MEDLINE
```sql
("atrial fibrillation"[Mesh] OR "atrial fibrillation"[Title/Abstract] OR AF[Title/Abstract])
AND ("cardiology"[MeSH] OR "heart disease"[MeSH] OR "cardiovascular"[Title/Abstract])
AND ("2019"[PDat] : "2024"[PDat])
```

### Semantic Scholar
```
Query: "atrial fibrillation" AND "cardiology"
Filters: publicationDate:>=2019-01-01, fieldsOfStudy:Medicine
```

### arXiv (for computational/methodological papers)
```
Query: "atrial fibrillation" OR "cardiac arrhythmia"
Categories: q-bio.QM, cs.LG, stat.ML
```

## Inclusion Criteria
1. Publication date: 2019-2024 (last 5 years)
2. English language publications
3. Peer-reviewed articles and high-quality preprints
4. Human studies or relevant computational methods
5. Cardiology focus or direct clinical relevance

## Exclusion Criteria
1. Non-English publications
2. Case reports (unless seminal)
3. Veterinary studies
4. Basic science without clear clinical translation
5. Publications before 2019

## Quality Filters
- Minimum citation threshold for established papers
- Journal impact factor consideration
- Peer review status verification
- Methodological rigor assessment

## Expected Results
- Target: 200-400 high-quality papers
- Clinical trials and guidelines
- Review articles and meta-analyses
- Computational methods for AF detection
- Treatment outcome studies
- Epidemiological research
