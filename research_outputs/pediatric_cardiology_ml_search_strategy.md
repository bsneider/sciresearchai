## Inclusion Criteria

- **Population:** Studies involving patients ≤18 years or pediatric-specific applications
- **Technology:** Machine learning, deep learning, or AI applications
- **Domain:** Cardiology, cardiac imaging, or heart disease management
- **Publication Date:** 2019-2024 (focus on recent advances)
- **Language:** English
- **Study Type:** Original research, validation studies, clinical trials

## Exclusion Criteria

- Adult-only studies without pediatric applications
- Pure theoretical papers without clinical validation
- Review articles (unless systematic reviews with quantitative analysis)
- Conference abstracts without full-text availability
- Non-English publications

## Quality Filters

- **Journal Impact:** Prefer high-impact cardiology and medical AI journals
- **Citation Count:** Prioritize well-cited research (≥5 citations for papers >1 year old)
- **Sample Size:** Prefer studies with adequate sample sizes for ML validation
- **Validation:** Require external validation or cross-validation methodologies
