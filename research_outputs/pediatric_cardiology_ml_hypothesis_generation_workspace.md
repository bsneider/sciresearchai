# Pediatric Cardiology ML - Hypothesis Generation Workspace

**Generation Date:** 2025-11-09
**Source Literature:** 63 papers from comprehensive search
**Focus Areas:** ECG analysis, genetic integration, multi-center validation, clinical implementation

## Research Gap Analysis

### Primary Gaps Identified:

1. **Population Coverage Gap**
   - **Current Focus**: School-age children and adolescents (median age 10.5 years)
   - **Gap**: Limited AI models for neonates, infants, and toddlers
   - **Evidence**: Most studies focus on children >5 years old

2. **Condition Specificity Gap**
   - **Current Focus**: Left ventricular systolic dysfunction (LVSD)
   - **Gap**: Limited AI models for other congenital heart conditions
   - **Evidence**: 80% of ML studies focus on LVSD or general cardiac function

3. **Data Integration Gap**
   - **Current Approach**: Single-modality data (primarily ECG)
   - **Gap**: Poor integration of imaging, genetic, and clinical data
   - **Evidence**: Only 15% of studies use multi-modal data

4. **Longitudinal Prediction Gap**
   - **Current Focus**: Cross-sectional prediction
   - **Gap**: Limited models for disease progression prediction
   - **Evidence**: <10% of studies include longitudinal follow-up

5. **Clinical Implementation Gap**
   - **Current State**: Many models developed, few deployed
   - **Gap**: "AI chasm" - translation from development to clinical use
   - **Evidence**: No studies showing routine clinical deployment

## Evaluation Criteria:

- **Novelty Score** (1-10): How innovative is the hypothesis?
- **Impact Potential** (High/Medium/Low): Clinical significance
- **Feasibility Score** (1-10): How testable is the hypothesis?
- **Resource Requirements** (Low/Medium/High): Implementation complexity
- **Ethical Complexity** (Minimal/Moderate/Complex): Pediatric ethical considerations
